Image and video transmission and compression

The work carried out in this area has been mainly oriented to systems for remote browsing of JPEG2000 images: the standard JPEG2000 has been deeply studied, the existing technologies and methods to build this kind of systems, within as well as outside the standard, have been analyzed and evaluated, and i have worked actively with the JPIP protocol (participation in the Google Summer of Code 2006 developing a Java client/server solution for JPIP and collaboration implementing this protocol for the Java Advanced Imaging API). Currently new methods (prefetching, transcoding, etc.) and protocols (JPIP-W, peer-to-peer, etc.) are being researched and developed in order to try to achieve the state-of-the-art of this kind of systems.

Another works have been carried out in this area, as for example the collaboration in the design of the FSVC (Fully Scalable Video Codec) video codec based on JPEG2000, and the development of efficient telepathology systems based on virtual slides with JPEG2000 (stitching methods and medical images transmission).


Control system engineering and automation. Image processing.

The work that is carried out in this area is oriented to the measure and test vehicles of the Tests Center of Michelin in Almería: design of autoguided vehicles, implementation of GPS fleet tracking solutions, control and measure systems based on CAN bus, etc. This work, due to confidentiality restrictions, can not be published.

The image processing field is also tackled, having developed several related solutions for the Tests Center of Michelin in Almería. These applications are used in order to get measures and information from tires by means of digital images.

Parallely, under the PhD program "Systems Engineering and Automation" at the National University of Education at a Distance, the use of the hibrid systems theory for the design of intelligent vehicles and multiagent solutions for traffic control is being researched.
